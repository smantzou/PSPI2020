Most efficient way to burn fat through working out

Have you tried everything and still nothing worked for you? Here are some tips for you to lose weight effectively.

By Healthy Life

Burning off excess fat can be quite a challenge for everyone. There are several factors that can influnce weight and fat loss, so in this article, we are about to discuss what can someone do in physical terms to achiece the "calories burnt > calories eaten" equation, but there is more to that. Even if you know what you have to do, it is not always easy to start doing it, in an efficient, for you, way. Here are 3 best ways to quickly burn fat and promote weight loss.



Start strength training

Strength training is doing body-weight exercises, lifting weights or using gym equipment. It is a type of exercise that builds muscle mass and increases strength and it has multiple health benefits, especially when it comes to burning fat. Strength training reduces visceral fat (a type of dangerous fat, that surrounds the organs in the belly) and if combined with aerobic exercises, it is more effective at reducing body and belly fat, than aerobic alone. Resistance training may also help preserve fat-free mass, which can increase the number of calories your body burns at rest. In conclusion, strength training has been shown to increase resisting energy expenditure and reduce belly fat, especially when combined with aerobic exercise.


Increase Cardio

As we said above, resistance training alone is not doing much in terms of losing excess body weight, but combined with cardio exercise (also known as aerobic exercise) it may lead to the best possible results. Cardio is one the most common form of exercises and is defined as any type of excercise that specifically trains the heart and lungs. The more aerobic exercise people are getting, the more belly fat they lose, according to 16 studies, and other studies have found that it can increase muscle mass and decrease belly fat, waist circumference and body fat. Most research recommends between 20-40 of cardio each day. Do a cardio exercise that fits you the most so you can keep doing it for as long as you can!


Try High-Intensity Interval Training

High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT), is a form of exercise that pairs quick burst of activity with short recovery periods to keep your heart rate elevated. HITT can be very effective on burning fat and promoting weight loss. One study found that people performing HITT for 20 minutes, three times weekly, lost 2Kg over a 12 week period, even with no other changes to their diet or lifestyle and they also experienced a 17% reduction in belly fat and decrease in waist circumference. HITT may also help you burn more calories in a shorter amount of time, than other forms of cardio (30% more calories burnt than other types such as cycling or jogging according to a study).



There are plenty of things available for you to do in order to shed excess fat and improve your health. There is no secret magic to it. All it requires is you, to take action and be patient! Keep in mind that its easier to start and follow a simpler change in your physical exercising, try to stay consistent, and increase your challenge rate weekly, so you don't get bored. But always remember! You cannot outwork a bad diet! If you decide it, the results are sure to come. Start now.
