Benefits of doing plank in daily basis

Plank, an easy exercise everyone can do, everywhere. Did you know how beneficial it is for your health?

By Healthy Life

Planks, are one of the most effective exercises, that you can do, anywhere, without any equipment needed and if you do it regularly, you can see results in a short period of time! They don’t require much time or effort to do and anyone, at any fitness level can do it. Here are some benefits of doing plank daily:



1. Your core strength will improve

One of the top benefits of doing planks regularly, is that they strengthen the core-muscles, bones and joints that link the upper and lower body. Their strengthening will give you an increased ability to lift heavy things, form a waistline and provide support for you back.


2. You will strengthen your back

While some exercises to work the core can actually weaken and potentially injure the back (such as sit-ups or crunches), the plank will actually help to strengthen it! It also, doesn’t add any unnecessary strain on the spine and can also reduce back pain, if they are done regularly , as well as providing strong support to your spine, especially its upper part. It can also improve your posture.


3. Your metabolism accelerates

Because you are engaging four major muscle groups (traverse abdominis, rectus abdominis, obliques and glutes), while trying to maintain and hold up your body, you challenge your body like this in short bursts. As a result, it increases your metabolism which helps you burn more energy when sedentary. A daily ten-minute workout, provides a higher level of metabolism throughout the day, even during sleep!


4. It improves your coordination and makes you more flexible

Doing plank exercises like the side plank, it will help you learn to keep your balance if performed regularly. Working out in this way, you stretch the muscles around shoulders, scapula and collarbones. Biceps and hip muscles also develop and your feet and toes become more flexible.


5. Your mood lifts up

The planking exercise affects your nervous system. It stretches and relaxes muscles which are usually stiffened due to stress, creating tension throughout the body. Plank also help to get rid of anxiety and symptoms of depression, if they are done in daily basis.



All in all, plank is an easy exercise that everyone can put on their daily workout routine and can be performed easily everywhere. It can boost your fitness level and benefit you in multiple ways. There is no reason for you to not get into doing planks regularly, so start now!
